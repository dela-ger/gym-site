ABOUT US

We are T3 Gym. We are a modern, state-of-the-art yet basic and affordable gym for people of all shapes and sizes.
Conceived initially by 3 friends (Mark, Dan and Luke) who had the idea of a gym with a modern presentation, look and feel. However, they didn't believe that access to all this should cost an arm and a leg, and share a strong belief of minimalism as well as making the most out of little. Thus, T3 Gym was born.
This unique concept brought forward by T3 Gym has created somewhat of a buzz in the fitness industry and we hope to continue increasing our reach as we strive for more recognition and maybe even expansion.
